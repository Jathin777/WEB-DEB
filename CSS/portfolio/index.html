The project on assessing renewable energy integration into the grid focuses on evaluating how various renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, and hydropower, can be efficiently incorporated into the existing electricity grid. It involves analyzing the technical, economic, and environmental impacts, addressing challenges like grid stability, energy storage, and intermittency of renewable sources. The goal is to ensure a reliable, sustainable energy supply while reducing carbon emissions and optimizing the use of renewable resources. This assessment will provide recommendations for enhancing grid flexibility and resilience.
